Welcome to the Internet!

The internet is an ever evolving and changing entity. We have come a long way from huge mainframe computers that only trained programmers knew how to use. We are still continuing forward with new and different devices. There is a whole slew of instruments that we have at our fingertips that can access the web and the programs that make them user friendly are ever changing and evolving as well, which makes the future a new and exciting place for innovation. On this site you can discover the process that made the internet what it is today.
